Motorcycle Maintenance

Keeping up with basic maintenance helps your motorcycle stay reliable, safe, and ready for the road.

Basic Maintenance Matters

Maintenance is not just about keeping the bike clean. It also helps prevent mechanical problems, improves safety, and gives riders more confidence before each ride.

Chain Maintenance

Clean and lubricate your chain every 300-500 miles. Always use a rear stand when cleaning, and make sure to clean the sprockets as well.

Oil Changes

Change oil every 3,000-5,000 miles or every 6 months. Riding aggressively may require more frequent changes.

Tires

Tires are your only contact with the road. Always check PSI, tread, and leaks before riding.

Engine & Valve Checks

Valve checks are usually around 15k miles and help ensure proper engine timing and performance.
